# Scene 1/3 - Arrival at the Academy

**POV:** Aria Moonwhisper
**Location:** Academy Gates
**Time:** Dawn

## Scene Summary

Aria arrives at the Celestial Academy for the first time. She's overwhelmed by the grandeur and the sheer number of students with magical abilities.

## Key Moments

1. **The Gates Open**
   - Massive iron gates inscribed with ancient runes
   - First glimpse of the towering spires
   - Hundreds of students flowing through

2. **First Encounter**
   - Bumps into Professor Thorne
   - He notices her unusual magical aura
   - Brief, cryptic warning: "Not everything here is as it seems"

3. **Orientation Hall**
   - Headmaster's welcome speech
   - Introduction to the four magical elements
   - Aria feels a strange pull toward the forbidden wing

## Emotional Beat

Aria is equal parts excited and terrified. This is her dream come true, but something feels... off.

## Foreshadowing

- Professor Thorne's warning
- The locked door in the forbidden wing
- Strange shadows moving in the periphery
