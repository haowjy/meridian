# Geography

Folder path is derived from the directory structure.

## The Five Kingdoms

### Northern Kingdom - Frostheim
- Climate: Frozen tundra
- Capital: Icehaven
- Known for: Ice magic, hardy warriors

### Eastern Kingdom - Solara
- Climate: Desert and oasis
- Capital: Sunspire
- Known for: Fire magic, scholars

### Southern Kingdom - Verdantia
- Climate: Tropical rainforest
- Capital: Greenleaf
- Known for: Nature magic, healers

### Western Kingdom - Aqualis
- Climate: Coastal and islands
- Capital: Tidehaven
- Known for: Water magic, sailors

### Central Kingdom - Terramount
- Climate: Mountains and valleys
- Capital: Stonekeep (home of the Academy)
- Known for: Earth magic, artificers
