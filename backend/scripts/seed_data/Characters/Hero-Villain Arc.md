# Hero-Villain Arc

This document tracks Aria's moral journey throughout the story.

## Act 1: The Hero

Aria starts as an idealistic young mage who believes in absolute good and evil.

- Saves innocents without question
- Follows Academy rules strictly
- Trusts authority figures

## Act 2: The Gray Area

After discovering the truth about the Academy's dark history, Aria questions everything.

- Makes morally ambiguous choices
- Realizes good people can do bad things
- Begins to understand villains' motivations

## Act 3: The Villain?

In the final act, Aria must make a choice that some will see as heroic and others as villainous.

**The Choice:**
She decides to destroy the Academy's power source to prevent future abuse, even though it means:
- Hundreds of mages will lose their abilities
- The protective barrier around the city will fall
- She'll be branded a traitor

## Resolution

Aria accepts that history will judge her harshly, but she believes it's the right thing to do. The story ends with her as both hero and villain, depending on who's telling the tale.
